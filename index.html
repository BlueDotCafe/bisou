Feeling at home with Bisou Patisserie

Expect to feel at home at Bisou in their warm and charming atmosphere. Established in 2015, these friendly bakers are committed to creating deliscious gluten-free pastries that are sure to please anyone. Even the most discriminating pallette will not be able to tell the difference between their GF treats to the most classic Parisian recipes. Our head Chef and founder trained in Paris at the famous Cordon Blu, after which she took years transorming the classics into, what are now, their best selling sweets. Now it truly is possible that no-one will go without!
